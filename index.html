💾 Data Backup Notice
This app saves data to your browser's local storage. That data can be lost if you clear your browser cache, switch browsers, or reset your device. Back up your game logs regularly.
  • How to back up: Open the app → tap the History tab on any saved game → use your phone's screenshot feature to capture the pitch log, or copy the pitch data to a note or spreadsheet.
  • Best practice: After each game, screenshot the Stats tab and the full pitch log before closing the app.
  • Switching devices? Data does not transfer automatically. Screenshot or write down pitch totals before switching.
